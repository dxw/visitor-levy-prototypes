Does your business have a trading name?

A trading name is the name a business operates under, which may be different from the name registered with HMRC when paying tax. It helps identify the business to customers and clients, especially in marketing or sales.
For example you might pay tax as Linda Ahmed, but your trading name is Blue Bay Cottages.
