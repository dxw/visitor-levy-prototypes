Register accommodation in Wales

When you start a new accommodation business or take over an existing business, you must register with this service. You should do this at least 7 days before people begin staying in paid accommodation.

Accommodation can include:

  • Bed and breakfasts
  • Campsites and caravan parks
  • Hotels
  • Self-catering

Registering takes around 5 minutes.
