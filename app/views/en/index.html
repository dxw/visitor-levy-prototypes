We are a team at the Welsh Revenue Authority, implenting an accommdation register and visitor levy in Wales.

This website documents how someone who provides accommodation in Wales might use public services. As well how different back-office system work.

The design work has been done in phases:

  • Register accommodation in Wales (autumn 2024)
  • Key moments end-to-end (spring 2024)
Autumn 2024 / Spring 2025

Register accommodation is Wales

The first service, people will have to register paid accommodation in Wales from 2026. The team aim in this phase, is to learn what the minimal viable registration service should include.

Spring 2024

Key moments end-to-end

We prototyped key moments across the first two years accommodation providers. Registering for the first time in 2026. Introduction of the visitor levy in 2027. And inevitably making changes in 2028, such as closing down a business.
