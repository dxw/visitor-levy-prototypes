We are a team at the Welsh Revenue Authority, implementing an accommodation register and visitor levy in Wales.

This website documents how someone who provides accommodation in Wales might use public services. As well how different back-office system work.

The design work has been done in phases:

  • Register accommodation in Wales (spring 2025)
  • Key moments end-to-end (spring 2024)
Spring 2025

Register accommodation is Wales

The first service, people will have to register paid accommodation in Wales from 2026. The team aim in this phase, is to learn what the minimal viable registration service should include.

  • sprint 4 - matching the legislation
  • sprint 5 - save and return
  • sprint 6 - business details: partnership
Sprint 6 - Feb 2024

Remote testing

Testing remotely with dozens of accommodation providers. Researched using web analytics and surveying people after they've tried the prototyped.

  • Business details: partnership 1.0
Sprint 5 - Jan / Feb 2024

Save and return

What does the serivce look like when returning to an incomplete registration.

  • Save and exit 1.0
  • Return to incomplete registration 1.0
Sprint 4 - January 2024

Matching the legislation

Few changes to the prototype last changed in March 2024. Making sure it the prototype collects all the data set out in the legislation.

The user journey for a sole trader was added, as the previous version just had add business details for a limited company. As well, manually entering business details, as they can't be pulled from a the Company House or Charity Commmission APIs for sole traders, who the team assumed will be the majority of users.

We prototyped key moments across the first two years accommodation providers. Registering for the first time in 2026. Introduction of the visitor levy in 2027. And inevitably making changes in 2028, such as closing down a business.
