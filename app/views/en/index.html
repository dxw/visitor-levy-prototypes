Visitor levy prototypes

We are a team at the Welsh Revenue Authority; tasked with implenting Wales' first ever visitor levy.

This website documents a potential timeline of someone who provide accommodation using public services.

Year 0

Register for the first time

  • Email from trade association
  • Start page followed by sign up and first registration
  • Confirmation email of new registration
  • Public record in accommodation register
Year 1

Local council starts a levy

  • Email about consultation by council
  • Email about introduction of local levy
  • Login page
  • Account homepage with notification banner
Year 2

Making changes to the register

  • De-register a property
  • Add more properties
  • Request to take on a property
  • Push notification trigged from Companies House
  • Stop being a liable person
